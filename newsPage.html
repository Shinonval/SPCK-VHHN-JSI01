Genshin Impact: How To Use Charlotte's Passive Talent (Special Analysis Zoom Lens Gadget Location)

Charlotte has a special passive talent but Genshin Impact players can only use it with a specific gadget


Some Genshin Impact characters have brilliant passive talents that can be immensely helpful while playing in the world of Teyvat. For instance, at least one character from every region has a passive talent that shows their region's local specialties on the Genshin Impact map , which makes the material collecting journey far easier. There are also characters that can save or obtain more resources, like units that get more reward when going on expeditions, or those that reduce the cost of upgrading or forging items. Among the interesting passive talents in Genshin Impact, Nahida likely has one of the best abilities. With her Elemental Skill, the Dendro Archon can capture materials from afar (typically plants), which can help greatly in collecting items that are on cliffsides and can be difficult to get manually. The ability also helps in reducing the time taken to approach each material on the map. Players can even use Nahida's Tap Skill to mass-collect items in the ability's range, reducing the amount of tapping required on some platforms, like PC.
